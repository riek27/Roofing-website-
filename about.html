About James Roofing

Houston's trusted roofing experts since 2003

Our Story

Company History

With over 20 years of experience serving the Greater Houston area, James Roofing has established a reputation for excellence, integrity, and superior craftsmanship. We don't just install roofs; we build peace of mind for homeowners and businesses alike.

Our team of certified professionals uses only the highest quality materials from trusted manufacturers to ensure your home or business is protected against the elements. From minor repairs to complete roof replacements, we treat every project with the same level of dedication and attention to detail.

Fully licensed and operating across Houston, Texas and surrounding service areas.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide Houston homeowners and businesses with reliable, high-quality roofing solutions that protect their investments and enhance property value. We are committed to exceptional craftsmanship, transparent communication, and long-lasting relationships.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted roofing contractor in Greater Houston, known for our integrity, expertise, and community involvement. We envision a future where every roof we install stands as a testament to quality and durability for decades to come.
